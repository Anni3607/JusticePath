JusticePath

JusticePath is an AI-powered legal awareness and assistance platform built using Python. It is designed to help ordinary citizens understand their rights, identify relevant authorities, generate complaint drafts, simplify legal language, and navigate the first steps of common legal issues.

The goal of JusticePath is not to replace lawyers or provide legal advice. Instead, it aims to make legal information more accessible, understandable, and actionable for people who may not know where to begin when facing a legal problem.

Problem Statement

Many people face situations such as online fraud, salary disputes, consumer complaints, tenant conflicts, or educational grievances. While legal information is available online, it is often scattered, difficult to understand, and intimidating for non-legal users.

JusticePath was built to bridge this gap by combining legal awareness, AI assistance, semantic search, and document generation into a single platform.

Features

Consumer Rights
Cybercrime Rights

Users can search by keyword or category to discover relevant protections, laws, and authorities.

Legal Problem Analyzer

Users can describe a legal issue in plain English.

The system:

Identifies the likely legal category
Suggests relevant authorities
Generates evidence checklists
Provides recommended next steps
Evidence Checklist Generator

Based on the identified issue type, JusticePath recommends the documents and evidence that should be preserved before filing a complaint.

Complaint Generator

Automatically generates professional complaint drafts that users can download as PDF documents.

Legal Language Simplifier

Converts complex legal language into plain English to make legal documents easier to understand.

Legal Research Assistant

Uses semantic search and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques to retrieve relevant legal information from the knowledge base and provide contextual guidance.

How It Works
Legal rights are stored in a structured JSON knowledge base.
User queries are classified into relevant legal categories.
SentenceTransformer embeddings are generated for legal knowledge entries.
FAISS retrieves semantically relevant information.
Gemini AI generates contextual responses and simplified explanations.
Complaint drafts and PDF documents are generated based on user inputs.
Challenges Faced
Building a Structured Legal Knowledge Base

One of the biggest challenges was organizing legal information into categories that could support multiple features such as search, classification, complaint generation, and retrieval.

Moving Beyond Keyword Search

Simple keyword matching often failed when users described issues differently. To improve retrieval quality, semantic embeddings and FAISS-based vector search were implemented.

Legal Language Simplification

Legal documents contain complex terminology that can be difficult for ordinary users to understand. Gemini AI was integrated to simplify legal language while preserving meaning.

PDF Generation Issues

Special characters generated by AI occasionally caused PDF export errors. Text sanitization and encoding fixes were implemented to improve reliability.
